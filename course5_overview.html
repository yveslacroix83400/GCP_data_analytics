Course 5: Put It All Together

Prepare for a Cloud Data Analyst Job

Welcome & Congratulations

You've reached the final course in the certificate program! This course is all about bringing together everything you've learned and preparing you for your first cloud data analyst role.

What You'll Review

  • Cloud storage & data management
  • Data processing & analysis
  • Data visualization in the cloud

What You'll Learn

This course will teach you how to prepare for a cloud data analyst job by:

  • Creating a professional resume
  • Building a portfolio project
  • Getting interview tips and strategies
  • Understanding job roles and expectations

Portfolio Project

Learn how to design and complete a portfolio project, and how to showcase your work to potential employers. You'll also learn how to claim your Google Cloud Data Analyst Certificate badge!
